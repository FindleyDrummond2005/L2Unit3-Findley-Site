Making my first song

This is a very personal achievement but I've decided to add it here because I believes it shows personal drive and creativity. I have spent the last five years playing instruments and occasionally writing music, and after a month or two of learning my first instrument (the ukulele) I wrote my own song with original chord progressions and lyrics. Even though it was a very rough song and needs major improvement I am very happy with all it taught me about perseverence, music writing, my own creativity, and more which is why I have placed it here.
